Installing Groups



You can install or uninstall entire groups at a time. To do that click on the group that you want to operate on and then right click your mouse. Then you will see a popup menu with options that you can perform. You can choose to install the selected group if it is not already installed. You can also uninstall the group if it is installed, or you can undo the last or all actions, or apply all changes.



From this point, you should see the Installing topic since the operations are identical.
